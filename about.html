About Joe's Pizza

Established in 1984, Joe's Pizza co. pride itself on serving an authntic Italian pizzas with a New York twist. Since opening their first restaurants at the Canaught Palaca in Delhi,Joe's has expanded into two or more restaurants distributed throughtout Delhi and NCR.

Our founder Joe Balochio has always said, "A great pizza starts with the selection of the fresh ingredients from local supplier ".This belief is emboded in the company and pizza that you will eat in our restaurants will never containn poor or cheap quality ingredients, we take great pride in the quality of our pizza.

Joe's Pizza co. is a member of National Restaurant Assocaition (NRA)
